# Free BLS/CPR Training Initiative

## 🚑 About
We are a student-led initiative dedicated to providing **free CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation) and Basic Life Support (BLS) training** to the community.

Our mission is to improve **emergency preparedness**, increase access to **life-saving skills**, and support **workforce readiness** for individuals entering healthcare careers.

---

## ❤️ Why This Matters
Sudden cardiac arrest can happen anywhere, and immediate action can save lives. However, many individuals lack access to affordable CPR/BLS training.

By removing cost barriers, we aim to:
- Increase the number of people trained in CPR
- Improve community response to medical emergencies
- Support individuals pursuing careers in healthcare

---

## 🎯 What We Do
- Provide **hands-on CPR and BLS training**
- Teach **AED (Automated External Defibrillator) use**
- Offer **accessible training sessions for the community**
- Support **healthcare workforce development through certification pathways**

---

## 📈 Impact Goals
- Train **hundreds of individuals annually**
- Expand access to **free CPR certification opportunities**
- Build partnerships with **hospitals, nonprofits, and community organizations**

---

## 🧠 Workforce Development
BLS certification is often required for healthcare roles such as:
- Medical Assistants (MA)
- Certified Nursing Assistants (CNA)
- EMTs and pre-health students

Our program helps individuals gain the credentials needed to enter the healthcare field.
