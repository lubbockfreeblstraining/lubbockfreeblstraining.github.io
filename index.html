Lubbock Free CPR/BLS Training

Free, community-based lifesaving education

Our Mission

Lubbock Free BLS Training is a medical student–led, volunteer community initiative dedicated to providing free, AHA-aligned Basic Life Support (BLS) and Heartsaver (CPR) training to students, community members, and individuals interested in learning lifesaving skills. Our mission is to improve emergency preparedness while expanding workforce readiness through accessible, high-quality education.

What We Offer

  • Free CPR & Basic Life Support training
  • Youth and community-focused instruction
  • Training delivered through authorized AHA Training Centers
  • Early exposure to healthcare and emergency response careers

Who We Serve

Our programs are open to individuals, classrooms, and community programs interested in learning lifesaving skills or exploring healthcare and emergency response careers.

Partners & Sponsors

We are actively seeking partnerships with hospitals, foundations, community organizations, and local businesses that share our commitment to improving emergency preparedness and workforce readiness in West Texas.
